Emergency Dental Services in Belleville



Oral pain or a chipped tooth, doesn't always happen at a convenient time.
At Belleville Dental Care, when you're experiencing a dental emergency
we will do our best to accommodate you, same day!


Contact Us Immediately

In a dental emergency, the sooner you see your dentist, the better. It may even be possible to save a tooth that has been knocked out if you seek treatment right away.

Our team is here to help provide you with treatment you need to help relieve pain and begin fixing your smile.

What is a dental emergency?

A dental emergency can include a severe toothache, broken tooth, loss of a crown or filling, knocked out tooth, or a sports injury to your teeth or gums. If you think you may need to see a dentist urgently for a dental emergency, give us a call or drop in.


Contact your Belleville dental team right away if you think you may have a dental emergency!
